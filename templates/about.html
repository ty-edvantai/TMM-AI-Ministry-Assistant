About This Project

TMM AI Ministry Assistant
Powered by retrieval-augmented AI and grounded in Scripture

This assistant was developed to support the mission of TMM — a ministry committed to deepening biblical understanding and spiritual growth through technology. It blends cutting-edge AI with verified biblical texts, commentaries, and theological resources to create a contextual, responsive study companion.

Designed to help users explore multiple versions of the Bible, cross-reference passages, and engage with theological works in real time, this assistant supports both personal devotion and scholarly inquiry.

Principles
• Operates within a framework of biblical integrity and theological respect.
• Maintains clarity, reverence, and intellectual rigor in all responses.
• Uses only verified sources — no fabrication or speculation.
• Defers doctrinal interpretation and pastoral guidance to ministry leaders.

This project reflects TMM’s commitment to responsible AI in ministry, showcasing how technology can illuminate Scripture, foster connection, and serve the Kingdom with wisdom and care.
